About Me

Hey, I’m Tejas — a passionate UI/UX designer and creative thinker who loves building digital experiences that feel smooth, meaningful, and human. I enjoy blending user psychology with clean interfaces to solve real problems, whether it’s through intuitive dashboards, serene productivity tools, or accessibility-focused redesigns. From minimal wireframes to polished prototypes, I handle the full design journey — and I’m always curious to learn, test, and improve.
