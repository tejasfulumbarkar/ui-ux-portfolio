Star Wars Merch App

Mobile App E-commerce UI/UX Sci-Fi

Project Overview

An eCommerce mobile application designed for Star Wars enthusiasts to explore and purchase themed merchandise like action figures, t-shirts, mugs, and more. The goal was to create an immersive, on-brand user experience that blends sci-fi aesthetics with a smooth shopping journey.

Objective

  • Resonates with the Star Wars fanbase
  • Offers intuitive navigation
  • Makes it easy to browse, wishlist, and purchase products
  • Aligns visually with the Star Wars universe

My Role

  • UI/UX Designer
  • End-to-end product design
  • Visual identity and UI system
  • Figma prototyping

User Research (Lightweight)

While no formal research was conducted, design decisions were inspired by:

  • Popular sci-fi themes
  • Common eCommerce UX patterns
  • Fan community feedback trends

Design Thinking Process

1. Empathize

  • Easy product discovery by category
  • Engaging visual style for fandom connection
  • Quick login/signup
  • Cart + wishlist for convenience

2. Define

  • Hero welcome page with brand punch
  • Clear navigation across categories
  • Product listings with visual focus
  • Smooth cart & checkout process
  • Wishlist & profile management

3. Ideate

  • A vertical scroll-based flow
  • Left-side navigation emphasis
  • High contrast UI with yellow, black, and white color scheme reflecting the Star Wars theme

UI Design

Visual Language

  • Color palette: Yellow accents over dark space-themed background
  • Fonts: Futuristic, bold for headings; clean sans-serif for body
  • Components: Rounded buttons, clean card layouts

Key Screens

  • Welcome/Login/Signup: Simple, dark UI with call-to-action
  • Homepage: Highlights "Hot Deals" and product categories
  • Menu: Clean side-drawer navigation with category links
  • Wishlist: Minimal, waiting to be filled
  • Product Pages: Grid-based layout for T-shirts, Action Figures, Mugs, and Bags
  • Cart/Checkout: Focused on clarity and ease

Design Decisions

Decision Reason
Dark theme Immersive and aligned with Star Wars feel
Bold yellow CTA buttons Improve visibility and flow
Grid product layout Fast visual scanning
Custom hero onboarding screen Instant brand connection

Usability Insights

  • Easy to scan layout
  • Large hit areas for mobile
  • Clear CTA placements
  • Familiar icons and navigation patterns
